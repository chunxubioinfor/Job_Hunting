# PhD Scholarship in Computational Immunology and Machine Learning
**Institution:** DTU Health Tech — Section for Bioinformatics
**Supervisor:** Associate Professor Leon Eyrich Jessen (ljess@dtu.dk)
**Deadline:** 2 April 2026
**Start date:** 1 June 2026
**Duration:** 3 years

## Role Summary
Design and develop ML frameworks for high-dimensional T-cell receptor (TCR) sequence data. Apply to large-scale public immune repertoire datasets and spatial transcriptomics data (in collaboration with Rigshospitalet). Translational research context: type 1 diabetes.

## Required Qualifications
- MSc in bioinformatics, computational biology, CS, applied math, or related (120 ECTS)
- Strong Python and/or R
- Documented ML experience for high-dimensional data
- Solid statistics and model evaluation
- Experience with biological sequence or omics data
- Excellent written and spoken English

## Key Tasks
1. Design and implement ML models for TCR sequence classification
2. Develop reproducible pipelines for large-scale repertoire analysis
3. Train/validate models on public immune repertoire datasets
4. Reconstruct TCR repertoires from spatial transcriptomics data
5. Evaluate model transferability across datasets and biological contexts
6. Benchmarking and comparative evaluation of ML models
7. Publications and international conferences
