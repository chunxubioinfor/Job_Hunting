# Junior Python Developer → Team Lead (Spiders & QA) - Rentumo

**Company:** Online Minds (Rentumo)
**Location:** Store Kongensgade, København K — On-site
**Type:** Full-time or part-time
**Salary:** Competitive

## Job Description

Rentumo is a rental housing search engine where automated Python scrapers scan 6,459 housing websites and find 38,833+ new listings every day across 10+ countries. Match notifications are sent via mobile/web app so users don't have to check hundreds of websites themselves.

The spider system is the core of the product — and this is where you come in. You start by learning the system in depth and building scrapers yourself, and over time get the opportunity to take responsibility and lead on it — and help develop and scale it.

## The Role

Plan: First 1–3 months you develop spiders yourself, learning the systems, tooling, and processes. Then you gradually get more responsibility with the opportunity to eventually become lead for the spider team.

**Team:** 6 Python developers (remote) + 4 QA specialists (remote)

Currently maintaining ~7,000 spiders, planning to build hundreds of new spiders globally in new markets and cities.

**Over time the role can evolve to include:**
- Coordination of spider development
- Improving QA flow
- Process improvements and documentation
- Recruiting new spider developers
- Technical responsibility for the spider area
