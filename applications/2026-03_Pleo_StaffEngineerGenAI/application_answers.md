# Pleo Application — Question Answers

## Tell us: Why Pleo? Why this job? And Why you?

**Why Pleo?**
I admire what Pleo has built out of Copenhagen. Turning something as messy as spend management into a product that 40,000+ businesses actually enjoy using is not easy. I have also noticed that Pleo is investing seriously in AI across the product, and that tells me this is a company where AI engineering is not just a buzzword but a real part of the roadmap.

**Why this job?**
I have been building LLM-powered tools on my own for the past year, and the GenAI Core role is basically the grown-up version of what I have been doing in my side projects: making AI infrastructure reliable so other teams can build on top of it. The idea of owning the platform layer, not just a single feature, is what makes this role stand out to me. I want to work where the engineering decisions I make have a multiplier effect across the whole product.

**Why me?**
I am not a staff engineer yet, and I know that. But I have built real systems that talk to LLM APIs in production, route between different model providers, and handle the messy parts like rate limiting, caching, and structured output parsing. I also have two years of experience maintaining open-source software used by people I have never met, which taught me a lot about building things that others rely on. I learn fast, I care about code quality, and I am genuinely motivated by this kind of work.
